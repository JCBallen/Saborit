TYPES OF LOAD

LESS THAN TRUCKLOAD (LTL)

Ideal for small cargos that do not require the vehicle to be fully loaded

Less Than Truckload shipping or LTL is the transportation of freight that combines shipments from multiple customers in a single truck.

FULL TRUCKLOAD (FTL)

Specialized for larger loads that require full size use of the truck

FTL or Full Truckload is the ground transportation service in which a complete truck is dedicated to a single load or, in other words, to a single customer.
